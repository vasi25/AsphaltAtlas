# AsphaltAtlas

A community platform for car enthusiasts to discover, share, and review scenic roads and driving routes.

## Concept

AsphaltAtlas lets drivers post and explore roads they've driven — offroad trails, mountain passes, lakeside routes, forest paths, coastal roads, and more. Think of it as a social map for road enthusiasts.

## Planned Features

- **User accounts** — register, log in, manage your profile
- **Route posts** — share a road with description, photos, and tips
- **Interactive map** — draw or trace your exact route on a map (displayed as a colored path)
- **Reviews & ratings** — 5-star rating system and comments from the community
- **Favourites** — save routes you want to drive or revisit
- **Categories** — filter by country, region, road type (offroad, mountain, coastal, forest, etc.)
